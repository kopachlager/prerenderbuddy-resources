# Prerender.io Alternative

Prerender.io is an established prerendering platform. Prerender Buddy is a smaller, simpler option focused on already-shipped JavaScript sites that need crawler-readable HTML.

## When A Simpler Alternative Makes Sense

Use a lightweight option when:

- You run a small SaaS, agency site, or no-code project
- You need a checker-first workflow
- You do not want to rebuild with SSR
- You want to solve crawler-readable HTML without a larger SEO platform

## When A Larger Platform May Fit Better

A larger platform may be better when:

- Rendering volume is high
- Enterprise support matters
- A dedicated SEO or engineering team owns the workflow
- Procurement and vendor maturity are important

## What To Compare

- Sites included
- Render limits
- Setup complexity
- Cache controls
- Reporting needs
- Support expectations
