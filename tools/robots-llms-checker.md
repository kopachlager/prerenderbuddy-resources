# Robots and llms.txt Checker

## What It Checks

This tool helps review crawler access files such as `robots.txt` and `llms.txt`.

## When To Use It

Use it when:

- AI crawlers appear blocked
- Search crawlers cannot access important pages
- `llms.txt` is missing or malformed
- You want to confirm basic discoverability files are present

## What The Results Mean

The tool checks access and formatting signals. It does not decide whether your content will rank or be cited.
