# Bot View Checker

## What It Checks

The Bot View Checker helps inspect what crawler-style requests receive from a public page.

It is useful for checking whether search engines and AI crawlers can receive readable HTML.

## When To Use It

Use it when:

- A page looks complete in the browser
- Search Console reports indexing or content issues
- AI crawlers may be receiving thin HTML
- You need to test root vs `www` behavior

## What The Results Mean

Good results suggest crawler-style requests can reach useful content. Weak results suggest bots may receive a thin shell, missing metadata, or too little readable text.

This tool does not promise rankings or AI citations.
